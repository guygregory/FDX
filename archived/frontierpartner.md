# Frontier Partner badge

## What is it?

The Frontier Partner badge is a new recognition introduced under the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program (MAICPP) at Ignite FY26. Here’s what it means and how it works:

- It’s a special badge designed to highlight partners who have achieved advanced capabilities in AI and modern solutions.
- The badge is not a designation like Solutions Partner; it’s an additional layer of recognition for partners meeting specific criteria.

## Eligibility

- Partners must achieve three specializations:
	- Microsoft Copilot
	- Build AI Apps, AI Platform, or Accelerate Developer Productivity
	- Data Security
- Partners must also attain Solutions Partner designations in:
	- Modern Work or Business Applications
	- Security
	- One of the three Cloud & AI Platform (Azure) aligned designations

## Purpose

- Provides visibility and differentiation in the Microsoft ecosystem.
- Signals to customers and resellers that the partner is at the forefront of AI and security innovation.

## Benefits

- Currently, it’s recognition only - there are no additional incentives or funding attached.
