# Frontier Program

## What is it?

- The Frontier Program provides direct access to Microsoft's latest AI innovations before they reach general availability.
- Participants can test breakthrough features, share feedback with product teams, and influence future development.

## Key Characteristics

- **Experimental Nature:** Features and agents under Frontier are in development and may change at any time.
- **Preview Terms:** All experiences are governed by Microsoft's preview terms under enterprise product agreements.
- **Hands-on Access:** Enables IT admins and users to explore new Copilot capabilities in real-world environments.

## Eligibility & Access

- Requires an active Microsoft 365 Copilot licence.
- Admins can selectively enable Frontier features for specific users or groups.
- No nomination process--features appear in the "Built by Microsoft" section of the app store when enabled.

## Benefits

- **Early Innovation:** Stay ahead by testing cutting-edge AI tools.
- **Feedback Loop:** Influence product design by sharing insights.
- **Competitive Edge:** Helps organisations shape mid-term GenAI strategies.

## Important Notes

- Features are preview-only, may lack localisation or support, and could include technologies that never reach GA.
- Initial rollout started in April 2025, expanding globally over time.
