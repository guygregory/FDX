# OpenAI Frontier

## What is it?

**OpenAI Frontier** is an enterprise platform announced by OpenAI that enables organisations to build, deploy, and manage AI agents across their business operations. Designed to bridge the gap between advanced AI capabilities and practical enterprise implementation, Frontier treats AI agents like members of the workforce—with onboarding, permissions, governance, and management tools.

## Key Features

- **Agents with Context:** Connects to internal databases, CRMs, data warehouses, and other applications to give agents the business context they need.
- **Onboarding and Permissions:** Each AI agent receives an identity, onboarding procedures, and tailored permission boundaries for secure, governed operation.
- **Agent Management:** Provides a dedicated environment for deploying, monitoring, and optimising agents, with feedback loops for continuous improvement.
- **Open Ecosystem:** Supports agents built by OpenAI as well as third-party agents from providers such as Google, Microsoft, and Anthropic.

## Why It Matters

- **Enterprise AI at scale:** Frontier is designed to move organisations beyond pilot projects to fully integrated, production-grade AI agent deployments.
- **Workforce transformation:** By treating AI agents as managed coworkers, Frontier introduces a new paradigm for how businesses structure and scale their operations.
- **Cross-platform compatibility:** Its open approach allows integration with a wide range of enterprise tools and agent providers.

## Early Adopters

Major companies including HP, Oracle, State Farm, Thermo Fisher, and Uber are among the first to pilot and adopt Frontier, highlighting its ability to reduce operational overhead, accelerate workflows, and scale AI agent deployments in complex environments.
