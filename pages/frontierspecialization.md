# Frontier Specialization

## What is it?

The **Frontier Specialization** is the next evolution of partner recognition for the AI agent era. It expands on the Frontier Partner badge and signals to customers and sellers that a partner can design, build, deploy, govern, and secure AI agents across the entire Microsoft Frontier Transformation stack.

The specialization is currently in private preview. Partners enrolled in the Frontier Partner badge will be invited to participate ahead of general availability.

## Partner requirements

Partners must attain four specializations:

- Copilot
- AI Apps or AI Platform (Microsoft Foundry)
- Data Security (Microsoft Purview)
- Identity and Access Management (Microsoft Entra)

Partners must also demonstrate:

- **Skilling:**
 - Five people with the new Frontier Transformation Engineer credential
 - Three people with the Fabric Analytics Engineer Associate certification (DP-600).
- **Agentic audit:**
 - Validation of the partner's capabilities to design, build, deploy, govern, and secure AI agents.

## Partner benefits

- **Customer and seller differentiation:** Stand out through the specialization badge, campaigns, and seller visibility.
- **Incentives and co-sell:** Gain access to incremental incentives and co-sell eligibility.
- **Product benefits:** Receive credits and licenses for building on the platform.
- **Marketing and go-to-market support:** Access packaged go-to-market programs and recognition.
