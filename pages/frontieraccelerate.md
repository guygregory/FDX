# Frontier Accelerate

## What is it?

**Frontier Accelerate** is Microsoft's unified partner investment program. It brings together previously separate offers, such as Azure Accelerate, into one cross-solution-area investment framework.

## Investment areas

- **Frontier Accelerate for Azure:** Aligns funding to strategic workloads across migration and modernization, data, and AI to drive Azure consumption. Because these funded engagements span the customer lifecycle, partners can scale impact while driving growth.
- **Frontier Accelerate for Copilot:** A hero investment across Microsoft 365 E7, Copilot, agents, and Agent 365. It supports discovery, technical workshops, and deployment motions that move customers from AI curiosity to operationalized Copilot and agent scenarios at scale.
- **Frontier Accelerate for AI-Ready Productivity:** Drives secure productivity attach and Microsoft 365 E3 and E5 renewal motions, helping partners protect and expand the install base that Frontier conversations build on.
- **Frontier Accelerate for Business Processes:** Empowers partners to land agent-led scenarios on Microsoft Dynamics 365 and drive strong customer outcomes.
- **Frontier Accelerate for Security:** A hero investment across Microsoft Security, identity, and data security workloads. It supports technical workshop delivery and accelerates usage across customer environments, helping partners strengthen protection and prepare customers for the agentic future.

## Value to partners

Together, these investments strengthen the core Microsoft 365 foundation while opening new opportunities for Copilot and agent growth. Partners who drive adoption and deepen customer engagement with strategic workloads can capture higher earnings and sustain stronger profitability. New Azure and per-user promotions, along with added margin, create fresh motions to scale consumption and growth.
