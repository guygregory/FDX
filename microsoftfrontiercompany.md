# Microsoft Frontier Company

## What is it?

**Microsoft Frontier Company** is a Microsoft operating business focused on delivering end-to-end Frontier Transformation through AI for customers around the world. It combines deep industry knowledge, change management and continuous improvement experience, and enterprise-grade AI engineering expertise.

## How It Works

- Embeds industry and engineering experts with customers to co-design, co-innovate, deploy, and continuously improve AI systems at scale.
- Builds intelligence platforms that help organizations compound the value of their proprietary data, expertise, workflows, and decision-making processes.
- Uses trusted platforms to observe, govern, manage, and secure AI solutions across the technology stack while measuring return on investment.
- Partners with the Microsoft ecosystem to extend this approach across markets and customer segments.

## Intelligence and Trust

Microsoft Frontier Company is built around two principles: amplifying customer intelligence and protecting it. Customer data, intellectual property, and competitive advantage remain protected and are not used to train models in ways that commoditize what differentiates the customer.

Its model-diverse, open AI platform also gives organizations flexibility to select the right model for each scenario without being locked into a single model or vendor.

## Investment and Scale

Microsoft is investing $2.5 billion in the organization and plans to embed 6,000 industry and engineering experts with customers. The organization is led by President Rodrigo Kede Lima.
